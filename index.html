About Me

My Teaching Philosophy

I believe mathematics is not just about numbers, but about developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills that last a lifetime.

With a student-centered approach, I focus on building strong conceptual foundations while making learning engaging and relevant to real-world applications. My goal is to transform the way students perceive mathematics - from a daunting subject to an exciting journey of discovery.
